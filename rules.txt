1.The aim of the game is to move the disks in tower 1 to tower 3 in minimum number of moves.
2.You can only place a smaller disc on top of the bigger one.
3.The right and left arrow keys in the keyboard helps traverse through the towers.
4.The up and down arrow keys will help you lift the disks and place the disks respectively.
5.Placing a disk counts as one move.
